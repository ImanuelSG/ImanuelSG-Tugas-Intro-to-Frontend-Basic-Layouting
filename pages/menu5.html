Monte Cristo Breakfast Sandiwch

Tools

  • -

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 artisan wheat bread slices any dense wheat bread works well
  • 2 tablespoon cream cheese
  • 3 slices deli black forest ham deli turkey, or both
  • 2 eggs fried (middles poked and spread out so the eggs lay flat)
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese
  • powdered sugar
  • raspberry Jam or any jam you like

Steps

  1. Mix eggs, maple syrup, milk, and vanilla together with a fork and pour in a shallow dish.
  2. Spread a good amount of cream cheese on each slice of bread. Add the ham and turkey, a fried egg, and a slice of Swiss cheese to one slice of bread and top with another slice of bread making a sandwich.
  3. Dip each sandwich in the egg mixture and turn over so the the entire outside of the sandwich is coated in the egg mixture.
  4. Cook on a skillet or in a frying pan over medium heat as if you were making French toast.
