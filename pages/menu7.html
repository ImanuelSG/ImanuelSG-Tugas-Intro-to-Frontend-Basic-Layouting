Ham, Egg, & Cheese Croissants

Tools

  • -

Ingredients

  • 2 bakery-fresh croissants
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices black forest ham sliced
  • mayonnaise to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook eggs over-easy (if you don't like runny yolks, break them while they are cooking).
  2. Place cheese over eggs.
  3. In the same pan heat up ham slices until they become nice and hot. Place hot ham over the cheese to help the cheese melt.
  4. Slice croissants in half and spread a little mayonnaise on each side.
  5. Place equal portions of the egg/ham on each croissant bottom. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, then add croissant tops.
  6. Serve immediately. They are the best when they are piping hot.
