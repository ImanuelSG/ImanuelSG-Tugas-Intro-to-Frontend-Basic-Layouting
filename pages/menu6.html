Foolproof Eggs Benedict

Tools

  • Blendtec Blender

Ingredients

  • 3 English muffins split in half (I used whole wheat)
  • 6 slices ham or Canadian bacon
  • 6 large eggs
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup unsalted butter melted and warm

Steps

  1. Fill a 12‚Äù lidded skillet nearly to the rim with water. Add the vinegar and 1 teaspoon salt and bring to a boil over high heat. Crack the eggs into coffee mugs, 2 per cup.
  2. Reduce the water to a simmer. Lower the rims of the mugs into the water and gently tip the eggs into the skillet simultaneously.
  3. Remove the skillet from heat, cover, and poach the eggs for 5 to 6 minutes.
  4. Blend egg yolks and lemon juice together in a blender until smooth and frothy, about 10 seconds.
  5. With the blender running, very slowly drizzle in the warm butter. Salt and pepper to taste.
